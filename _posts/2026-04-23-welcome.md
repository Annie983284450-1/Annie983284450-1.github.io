After a long stretch of the old template sitting untouched, I finally took a weekend to refresh this site. A quick tour of what changed and what's next.

## What's new

- A proper **home page** — hero section, selected publications, selected projects, and a news feed. Before, the front page was just a bio and an education list.
- A **cards layout** for publications and projects. Each card shows venue, year, and direct links to the PDF, slides, or code where available.
- **Real mobile support**. The old two-column layout stacked awkwardly on phones. Now the home has a dedicated mobile hero, and inner pages use a compact inline sidebar.
- **Better typography** — *Inter* for UI, *Source Serif 4* for long-form reading. Font sizing scales fluidly from phones to desktops.
- A **blog**, which is what you're reading now. I'd like to use this space for research notes, quick reading lists, and the occasional longer post on uncertainty quantification and RL in healthcare.

## Why write here

I'm usually more comfortable writing papers than blog posts, but a paper isn't the right format for half-finished ideas, recipes that worked, or an hour-long rabbit hole that deserves a short note. This is the space for those.

## What's next

- Seed a few more posts from notes I've been collecting on conformal prediction and contextual bandits.
- Add a talks/presentations page (the current "Certifications" page is a little thin).
- Image optimization and an OG card for each post.
