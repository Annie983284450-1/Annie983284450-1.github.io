My Ph.D. dissertation develops a unified framework for early sepsis prediction that provides calibrated, actionable uncertainty bounds at the bedside. The work threads together three research contributions: (1) **OnAI-Comp**, an online AI-experts competing framework with average-regret guarantees; (2) **Sepsyn-OLCP**, a gap-based online learning algorithm paired with split conformal prediction; and (3) **NeuroSep-CP-LCB**, a neural contextual bandit that directly models patient-specific reward functions with conformal confidence bounds. Together these address the central challenge of ICU decision-support: models that are both accurate and *honest* about when they shouldn't be trusted.
