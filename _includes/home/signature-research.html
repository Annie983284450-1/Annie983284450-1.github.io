Signature Research — Uncertainty-Aware ML for the ICU All projects →

Three papers, one pipeline. Each builds on the last — from the peer-reviewed foundation through two 2025 preprints — and each ships with open-source code.

Step 01 2021 · IEEE/ACM TCBB

OnAI-Comp

Online experts compete — and a trusted selector picks the winner per patient.

Heterogeneous ML experts (XGBoost, RF, LR, …) are evaluated online by average regret; the selector converges to the best expert in hindsight, and LIME attributions keep the choice auditable for clinicians.

Step 02 2025 · arXiv

Sepsyn-OLCP

Wrap the pool in split conformal prediction. Select experts by gap, not accuracy.

Every prediction gets a distribution-free confidence interval with guaranteed marginal coverage; gap-based selection degrades gracefully when a single expert is confidently wrong.

Step 03 2025 · arXiv

NeuroSep-CP-LCB

Drop the expert pool. Model the reward directly with a neural net — safely — using a conformal LCB bonus.

An LCB exploration bonus penalises extrapolation into regions the logged policy never visited; conformal prediction calibrates the bonus width rigorously instead of tuning it heuristically.
